Pattern Bullish and Bearish Engulfing + Tweezers + Morning/Evening Stars

Indicator to Identify a few Candlestick Pattern.

Indicator to identify Bullish and Bearish Engulfings, Tweezers and Morning/Evening Star

Bullish Engulfing: formed when a small bearish candle is followed by a large bullish candle that completely engulfs the previous candle.
Bearish Engulfing: formed when a small bullish candle is followed by a large bearish candle that completely engulfs the previous candle.
Tweezer Bottom: formed by two or more consecutive candles with matching lows.
Tweezer Top: formed by two or more consecutive candles with matching highs.
Morning Star: formed by a small bearish candle, a large gap down, and a bullish candle that closes above the midpoint of the first candle.
Evening Star: formed by a small bullish candle, a large gap up, and a bearish candle that closes below the midpoint of the first candle.

  • Chart: Bar, Candle, Heikin Ashi
  • Category: Indicator
